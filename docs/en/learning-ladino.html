Learning Ladino

Mobile Applications to learn Ladino

  • utalk has a Ladino course with voice.

Free Ladino Courses on YouTube

  • Ladino 101

    • Rachel Amado Bortnik
    • Video recordings

  • Ladino 101 - A Beginner's Course of The Sephardic Jewish Brotherhood of America

    • Video recordings
    • Karen Sarhon

  • Vini Echaremos Lashon - Ladino course on YouTube using Hebrew as the language of explanation. By the Autoridad Nasionala del Ladino i su Kultura

  • Curso online de iniciacion al ladino - a 3-part course for Spanish speakers.

Books to learn Ladino

  • Beginner's Ladino by Alla Markova

Courses and private lessons

  • Ladino 21 has short (4x1 hour) courses in Ladino with Alejandro Acero Ayuda and/or with Carlos Yebra López. They also offer private classes.

  • eSefarad has short (4x1 hour) courses in Ladino with Liliana Benveniste.

  • Ladino Linguist has a long 16-session 20 hours course with Bryan Kirschen.

  • italki has no Ladino teachers. However I exchanged email with their support and as they have Ladino listed on their site one can apply to be a teacher or a community tutor.

Studying Ladino

There are several Universities that offer Ladino courses in some way:

  • University of Washington
  • Hebrew University in Jerusalem
  • Salti Institute at Bar-Ilan University in Ramat Gan
  • The Moshe David Gaon Center for Ladino Culture at Ben-Gurion University in the Negev
